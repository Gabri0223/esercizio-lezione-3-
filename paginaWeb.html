Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350gr) lukewarn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil. for brushing on top
  • 10 ounes brie,cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, thougth it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

    Make the focaccia dought

In a medium-sized mixing bowl or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dought ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
